The Govno Core 32-20020 has 34 registers in total.
These include 32 general purpose registers, which can be accessed by these names:
  EAX, EBX, ECX, EDX, ESI, EDI, ESP, EBP, E8 - E31
and aliased by
  E0 - E31 or R0 - R31,
Processor Status Flags, or PS,
and Program Counter, or EPC.
